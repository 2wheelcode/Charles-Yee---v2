About Charles

Charles is an industry leader in healthcare NLP (Natural Language Processing) with nearly two decades of experience building commercial products, both in a hands-on capacity and as a technical manager.

As a player coach, Charles has led multimillion-dollar projects in health-tech Fortune 500s such as United Health, Philips, and AstraZeneca.

Charles was also the co-founder and early employee of multiple Series-A and Series-B startup companies.

Charles has a Ph.D. in computational linguistics. With over a dozen publications and patents in his field, Charles is an authority in machine learning for named entity extraction and classification.

Charles’ expertise ranges from semantics, ontology, machine learning, and deep learning approaches for information classification and extraction.
Providing leadership, orchestration, and implementation to materialize domain experts’ scientific rationale into commercial products.
Uniquely versed in clinical trial protocol, oncology/pathology, EMR/EHR, and NLP-Assisted ICD coding.
